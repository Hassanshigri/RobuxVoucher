Privacy Policy

At RobuxVoucher, your privacy is our priority.

At RobuxVoucher, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you interact with our platform. By using RobuxVoucher, you agree to the terms described in this policy. We are committed to transparency and ensuring your data is handled responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information: personal and non-personal. Personal data includes your name, email address, payment details, and account credentials, which you provide when signing up, purchasing Robux, or contacting support. Non-personal data includes information automatically gathered via cookies or analytics tools like your IP address, device type, operating system, browser, and site behavior (pages visited, clicks, time spent).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide, manage, and improve our services. This includes processing Robux transactions, delivering personalized gaming recommendations, sending updates or promotional offers, and responding to inquiries. We also analyze usage patterns to enhance site performance and detect fraud or abuse. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your data with third-party service providers such as hosting companies, payment processors, or analytics partners but only to perform tasks on our behalf. These providers are bound by strict data protection obligations. In some cases, we may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of RobuxVoucher and its users.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

RobuxVoucher uses cookies and similar tools to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and understand user preferences. These help us remember you across sessions and tailor content accordingly. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. For more, please review our Cookies Policy.

5. Data Security

We take your data security seriously. RobuxVoucher uses encryption, firewalls, and secure servers to protect your personal data. While no online system is 100% secure, we implement industry-standard measures to reduce risks. We also limit access to your data to only authorized personnel.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You can request a copy of your data or withdraw consent to data processing at any time by contacting us at info@robuxvoucher.com. We will respond to requests in accordance with applicable laws, typically within 30 days.

7. Children’s Privacy

RobuxVoucher is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected without verified parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Our services may involve the transfer of your information to servers or partners located in other countries. Where required by law, we ensure such transfers are protected using approved legal mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our practices. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our services after updates indicates your acceptance of the revised policy. The “Last Updated” date will always reflect the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, your personal information, or your rights, please contact our support team at info@robuxvoucher.com or visit our Help Center. We value your trust and are here to help.
